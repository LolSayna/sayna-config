- Hwmonitor or *Hwinfo* - System Information

- GpuZ - Gpu Stats
- *Crystal disk info* or Western Digital Dashboard or Disk Stats or HD Tune - Disk Stats
- Windirstats or Treesizefree - Manage free disk space
